About This Project

NeuroAid is an intelligent Medical Diagnosis & Healthcare Assistant designed to make healthcare more accessible, efficient, and reliable. By leveraging Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, NeuroAid helps predict possible diseases from symptoms and medical images, offering quick insights that support patients and healthcare professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between patients and early healthcare by providing a user-friendly platform that delivers instant health assistance, predictive insights, and secure health record storage.

What We Offer

  • Disease prediction from symptoms with high accuracy.
  • Integration of health record storage for easy access.
  • AI-powered chatbot for instant healthcare support.
  • Interactive dashboards for tracking accuracy and patient engagement.
  • Continuous improvement with advanced data processing pipelines.

Why Choose NeuroAid?

Unlike traditional systems, NeuroAid focuses on speed, accuracy, and accessibility. It empowers users with the ability to get medical insights in seconds, helping doctors and patients make better-informed decisions.

Future Goals

We aim to expand NeuroAid into a complete digital healthcare ecosystem with features like real-time patient monitoring, multilingual support, and integrations with wearable devices for preventive healthcare.
